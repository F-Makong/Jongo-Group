ABOUT US


Welcome to Jongo Group, your gateway to innovation and technology empowerment. We are a dynamic ecosystem dedicated to fostering creativity, supporting startups, and empowering individuals to thrive in the digital age. Fueling Growth through innovation and building tomorrow's Leaders.


JONGO HUB


At Jongo Hub, we are committed to driving impactful change through innovation and entrepreneurship. As an innovation-based tech-driven enterprise support structure, we empower people, ventures, and communities for high impact. Our initiatives include:


  • Code Reactor Program: Our premier program bridging the tech and skill gap by providing immersive learning experiences and real-world work opportunities for talented interns.

  • Startup and SME Clinic: A specialized support system guiding startups through various stages of development.

  • Multimedia Content Production Studios: Equipped with cutting-edge technology for creating compelling narratives that inspire and educate.

  • Tech Lab: An innovation hub providing tools and resources for turning ideas into reality.


Jongo Hub Academy


Jongo Hub Academy is our tech-based training academy, offering mentorship-driven programs that equip aspiring entrepreneurs and tech enthusiasts with essential digital and business skills. Our academy focuses on:


  • Skill Development: Tailored courses ranging from six months to one year, strengthening core foundations and fostering a community of industry experts.

  • Social Impact: Prioritizing startups with high social impact to address community challenges through innovative technological solutions.

  • Collaborative Learning: A supportive environment where like-minded individuals collaborate and grow together.
